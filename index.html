The Sacred Land of Naimisharanyam

Naimisharanyam is a place of immense importance in Hindu belief, considered the very first pilgrimage site on Earth. Its holiness comes from a divine event where Lord Vishnu's Sudarshana Chakra is said to have landed here, creating the sacred pond known as Chakra Tirtha. This act immediately sanctified the land, marking it as a special place for worship and spiritual practice. Furthermore, this sacred forest is famous as the place where knowledge was given to the world.
